### Step 3: Manual on-device verification

1. Clear app data (or use a fresh emulator snapshot) to reset SharedPreferences.
2. Launch the app — verify onboarding dialog appears immediately.
3. Tap outside dialog — verify it stays open.
4. Press back button (Android) or swipe from left edge (iOS) — verify dialog stays open.
5. Swipe on slide content — verify no slide transition.
6. Tap "Next" on slide 1 → slide 2 appears. Tap "Next" on slide 2 → slide 3 appears.
7. Verify "Got it" button appears (not "Next") on slide 3.
8. Tap "Got it" → dialog closes, app is fully usable.
9. Close and reopen app — verify no dialog on second launch.

### Step 4: Asset-swap drill (verify FR-3 configurability)

1. Replace `assets/onboarding/slide_1.png` with a different PNG of the same name.
2. Run `fvm flutter run` (hot restart or rebuild).
3. Verify the new image renders on slide 1 with no code changes required.
4. Restore original placeholder.

Verify no new packages appear beyond what existed before this epic.
